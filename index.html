Kangaroo Run

Your goal in Kangaroo Run is to collect as many berry bushes as possible while avoiding rocks.

How to play:
goal of the game is to gain the highest Score.
To increase you score you must collect the berry bushes.
You must also avoid the rocks, Touching any of the rocks will result in the game ending.

Controls:
Left click to Jump.
